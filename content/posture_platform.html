Posture Platform

The Posture Platform is a collaborative project between SPIN developers and Luc Courchesne based at the Société des arts technologiques [SAT]. Below is a video that explains the general concept behind the project. More information can be found on the Posture website.

Exhibitions / Events / Installations:

McLuhan’s Massage Parlor

Toronto's Nuit Blanche 2011
October 1-2, 2011

Inspired by McLuhan’s books “The Medium is the Massage” and “Counterblast”, this immersive and participatory installation celebrates McLuhan’s insights in a medium he could only dream about.To be presented as part of Nuit Blance, Toronto.

Follow the MEDIA McLUHAN development blog for more information.
